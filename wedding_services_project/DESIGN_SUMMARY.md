# Wedding Services Platform - Design Summary

## üé® Design Philosophy

The Wedding Services Platform features a **romantic, elegant, and modern** design that reflects the special nature of wedding planning. The design balances sophistication with usability, creating an experience that feels both luxurious and approachable.

## üåà Color Scheme

### Primary Palette: Romantic Rose
- **Main Color**: `#ec4899` (Primary 500)
- **Purpose**: Primary actions, brand identity, key highlights
- **Psychology**: Evokes romance, elegance, and celebration

### Accent Color: Elegant Gold
- **Main Color**: `#f59e0b` (Gold 500)
- **Purpose**: Premium features, special offers, luxury elements
- **Psychology**: Suggests luxury, quality, and special occasions

### Supporting Colors
- **Cream/Beige**: Warm, elegant backgrounds
- **Gray Scale**: Professional, clean text and borders
- **White**: Purity, cleanliness, modern feel

## üìê Typography Hierarchy

### Display Font: Playfair Display
- **Usage**: Headings, hero text, elegant statements
- **Style**: Serif, sophisticated, wedding-appropriate
- **Example**: "Find Your Perfect Wedding Service"

### Body Font: Inter
- **Usage**: All body text, buttons, navigation
- **Style**: Sans-serif, clean, highly readable
- **Example**: Service descriptions, form labels

## üì± Responsive Design Strategy

### Mobile First Approach
- Touch-optimized buttons (minimum 44px)
- Simplified navigation (hamburger menu)
- Stacked layouts
- Full-width cards

### Tablet Optimization
- 2-column grids
- Side-by-side forms
- Medium-sized images

### Desktop Experience
- 3-4 column grids
- Sidebar filters
- Larger hero sections
- Hover interactions

## üé® Component Design Language

### Buttons
- **Primary**: Rose background, white text, rounded corners
- **Secondary**: White background, rose border, rose text
- **Gold**: Gold background for premium features
- **Hover**: Scale effect, darker shade, shadow elevation

### Cards
- **Service Cards**: Image header, rounded corners, soft shadow
- **Feature Cards**: Icon, title, description, cream background
- **Stat Cards**: Large number, label, colored accent

### Forms
- **Inputs**: White background, rose focus ring
- **Labels**: Medium weight, gray text
- **Validation**: Red error text, green success

### Navigation
- **Navbar**: White background, sticky, subtle shadow
- **Links**: Gray default, rose on hover/active
- **Icons**: Font Awesome, consistent sizing
